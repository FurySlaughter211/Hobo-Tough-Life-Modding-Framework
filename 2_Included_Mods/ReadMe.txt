This folder contains mods I created so you can have a reference of what works. I'm also planning to make Document explaining exactly how to use this but its gonna take some time. 

This folder currently contains 3 mods:

"Consumables": This is a mod that spawns a custom item called Junkies Juice. It can be spawned with F5 when the HoboMod.DevTools.dll is installed or also naturally spawns in packs of 5 in the ashtray at the train station with a 	       very high probability of spawning. It gives you 50 health and applies the healing and the confidence buff. 


"Gear": This mod adds a hat called conspiracy crown into the game. It can only be spawned with F6 currently while HoboMod.DevTools.dll is installed. 
        It gives you -60 charisma, 40 Immunity and 30 toxicity resistance. Currently the -60 charisma are properly applied but the game doesn't know how to deal with negative stats so its handled like you have +60 charisma.
        I will fix that at some point but if you actually want to use this item you can just change the values in tinfoild_fedora.json

"SkinnedMeshReplacement": This mod replaces Maisner with a Zombie. Be warned though. This is not fleshed out a lot yet. He currently replays his zombie animations over and over again, because of that slowly walks into a wall and I 		          found a bug where other NPCs get replaced for no obvious reason. 

WARNING: I WOULD RECOMMEND PLAYING THE GAME WITH THESE MODS. THEIR ENTIRE PURPOSE IS TO BE A PROOF OF CONCEPT.
